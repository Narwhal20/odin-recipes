Chicken and Rice Bowel Recipe

What is it?

A chicken and rice bowl is a versatile and nutritious dish that combines tender, seasoned chicken with fluffy rice and a medley of fresh vegetables. This dish is perfect for a quick lunch, a weeknight dinner, or meal prep for the week. The beauty of a chicken and rice bowl lies in its simplicity and adaptability, allowing you to customize it with your favorite flavors and ingredients.

This recipe features marinated chicken cooked to perfection, paired with a fragrant rice base, and topped with a colorful array of vegetables. The marinade infuses the chicken with a delightful blend of spices, while the vegetables add freshness and crunch. Finished with a drizzle of a flavorful sauce, this bowl is a wholesome, delicious meal in one

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1-2 cups of assorted vegetables
  • Sauce
    • 2 Tbs soy sauce
    • 1 Tbs rice vinegar
    • 1 Tbs honey
    • 1 Tsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Prepare Chicken
  2. Cook rice, accoring to your rice maker or pressure cooker
  3. Cook chicken in skillet over medium heat
    • Heat 1 Tbs of olive oil
    • Cook chicken for 5-7 minutes per side, or internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F
  4. Cook vegetables, according to packaging
  5. Make sauce, combine Ingredients in a bowel and mix
  6. Combine all ingredients in desired amounts and serve
