BBQ Ribs Recipe

What is it?

BBQ ribs are a quintessential comfort food that combines smoky, savory flavors with tender, fall-off-the-bone meat. Whether you're cooking for a summer cookout or a cozy family dinner, BBQ ribs are sure to please everyone at the table. This recipe focuses on creating ribs that are both flavorful and succulent, using a combination of a dry rub, slow cooking, and a tangy barbecue sauce.

The preparation starts with a well-balanced dry rub that infuses the meat with a depth of flavor. The slow cooking process ensures the ribs are tender and juicy, allowing the connective tissues to break down perfectly. Finally, the ribs are slathered with a rich barbecue sauce and grilled to caramelize the sugars, giving them a beautiful, sticky glaze.

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of pork ribs
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 cup of BBQ sauce
  • Simple dry rub

Steps

  1. Preheat ovin to 275 degrees F
  2. Prepare ribs for dry rub
  3. Apply olive oil and dry rub
  4. Place on a lined baking sheet, wrap in aluminum foil, bake for 2.5 to 3 hours
  5. Prepare grill, set to medium-high heat, or boriler in the oven
  6. Unwrap, apply BBQ sauce, place in grill or boiler 5-10 minutes each side
  7. Serve
